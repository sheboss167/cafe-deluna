Cafe Deluna

Cafe Deluna is family restraunt that was established in 2010, where it grew from a small cafe to now one of the largest cafes in the country. It is located in lavington Nairobi.
Its decor is inspired by the rich diverse Kenyan culture and also still accomodates the growing nature of the country.
